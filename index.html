Voice Analyzer

Upload a sustained vowel recording to get instant professional acoustic analysis with detailed measurements and clinical interpretation.

How it works: Record yourself saying "aahhh" (like at the doctor's office) for 3-5 seconds at a comfortable pitch. The analyzer will measure fundamental frequency, jitter, shimmer, harmonics-to-noise ratio, and cepstral peak prominence—the gold standard parameters for acoustic voice quality assessment.

⚠️ Important: Use sustained vowel recordings only (/a/, /i/, or /u/), not running speech or singing. Jitter and shimmer measurements require continuous phonation to be clinically valid.

Note: Files longer than 30 seconds will be automatically trimmed to the first 30 seconds for memory-optimized analysis.

📝 Recording Tips for Best Results

✅

What to Record

Say "aahhh" steadily for 3-5 seconds at your normal speaking pitch

🎤

Recording Quality

Quiet room, normal volume, 6-12 inches from microphone

🔇

What to Avoid

No speech, singing, pitch changes, or background noise

📱

Any Device Works

Phone voice recorder, computer mic, or any recording app
